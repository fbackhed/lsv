Svalbard global seed vault acts as a backup on the worlds seeds and was build to be independent without human help and last for a long time. There have already been an impact from climate change as permafrost been melting. The consequenses of climate change have led to an upgrade of the seed vault and a need of humans at the vault 24/7.

Ideas about the project came up when questions was asked if svalbard global seed vault will survive in the future. The projects solution to this question is a seed vault on the moon with a backup of the seeds on earth. By having the seed vault on the moon the seeds are rescued if something bad is happening on earth as climate change, virus or collision from astroids. In the beginning of the project research was done about how the physical of todays internet look and what the climate change consequenses are and how it can be build better in the future.

For the project a branding was created with the visual identity to be used on this website.
