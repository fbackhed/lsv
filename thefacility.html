PROJECT             ABOUT            SEEDS            CONTACT



The facility is located on the moon to protect seeds from future threats on planet Earth. Japanese scientist was the first to find the underground lava tubes on the moon which may hold a suitable environment for a seed vault. The lava tubes can protect the seed vault against radiation from outer space, falling asteroids and extreme temperatures. The temperature is more stable in the tubes with a temperature between -30 and -40 degrees which can help the storage of the seeds.
