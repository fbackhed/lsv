PROJECT  ABOUT SEEDS CONTACT



The seeds of Lunar Seed Vault is well protected and will be used in future science about growth in space and history of earth. By having access to the seeds on the moon this can help feed the moonbase without long transports from the earth.

The major depositers of the seed vault is genebanks as the international research centres of the consulative Group on International Agricultural Research (CCGIAR), with the International Maize and Wheat Improvement Center (CIMMYT), International Center for Agricultural Research Institute for the Semi-Arid Tropics (ICRISAT), International Rice Research Institute (IRRI) and the International Center for Tropical Agriculture (CIAT). With Genebanks in Netherlands, Canada, Germany and Usa as the largest National depositors. While the Nordgren which is a gene bank for the Nordic countries is the main regional depositor.
